PacMen_Factory

The PacMen Factory is a simple program that allows the user to make mulitple PacMen and then have them randomly zoom around the screen. Press the "Add PacMen" button to create as many PacMen as desired and then press "Start Game" to see them randomly fly across the page at different speeds.

Eyes

The Eyes project has a set of eyes in the window that follow the movements of the cursor across the screen making it appear as though they are watching the movements.

Real Time Bus Tracking

The Real Time Bus Tracking exercise shows the bus stop animation from Stony Brook University (SBU) to Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). There are markers showing where both MIT and Stony Brook University are located. Click the button "Bus Map from SBU to MIT" and the marker follows the bus route up to MIT.
